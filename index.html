Coldest Trollface Moments

In the history of internet culture, trolls have become symbols of provocation, humor, and irony. It all started with the legendary “Trollface” meme, which first appeared in 2008 and was created by Carlos Ramirez. This character became an icon of deliberate jokes designed to provoke strong reactions. Now, in the era of Web3 and memecoins, the troll figure has made a comeback as a symbol of unpredictable communities that often defy expectations. Troll memes are not just jokes — they represent a digital rebellion against the mainstream. This project is a tribute to the cultural power of trolls in the age of social media. Below, you'll find a collection of the most viral, legendary, and hilarious troll images from the internet — completely free and used by millions of people around the world.
